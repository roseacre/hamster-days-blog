About Us

I'm a young, male Syrian hamster, called Milku.

Currently, I'm enjoying life in the North-West of England, getting used to living with a family of humans in Blackpool.

My current ambition is to train my humans to stop giving me the yucky yellow bits in the hamster muesli. What is that stuff?!

I spend my days sleeping. What else!

My nights are a glorious mix of playing and feeding... and keeping a wary eye on the insomniac humans who keep checking in on me.
