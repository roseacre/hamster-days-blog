A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

They're all together ooky.

15-May-2017 | Milku


But they are my human family.

I'm bracing myself for the International Day for Families. While I've lost contact with my birth family, my adopted human family will probably want to play with me. Provided today's celebrations involve a bit of cake or a special treat, I'll go along with it.

After all, my family do provide some amusement. Two of the little humans went along to a Rookie Life Saving class on Friday. But only one took clothes to change into afterwards. Oops! Fortunately she managed to come home without celebrating National Nude Day prematurely... thanks to her Mum.
