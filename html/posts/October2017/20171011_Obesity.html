Obese?

11-Oct-2017 | Milku


Not me...I'm just well-furred.

Apparently, today is Pet Obesity Awareness Day. Well, I'm a fine figure of a hamster. No problems with pet obesity in my house. Maybe my two legged friends should concentrate on their own BMI values and not look for issues in my cage...

I've just been made aware that there are 75 sleeps until Christmas. I'll have to write my gift list.

Surprisingly, my little humans haven't started looking for the Argos catalogue yet so they can get some ideas... won't be long though...
