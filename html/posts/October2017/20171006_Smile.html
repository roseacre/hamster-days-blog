A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

Do an act of kindness.

06-Oct-2017 | Milku


Help one person hamster smile.

It's World Smile Day today.

I enjoy smiling as must as the next hamster, but it always helps if I get an extra special treat in my bowl...

I actually smiled quite a lot last night when I discovered a portion of fresh corn on the cob had been laid out for me. The dried stuff is great, but...Wow!...the fresh kernels are just delicious. I was glad the humans didn't disturb me during my waking hours as I had to gnaw my treat in situ. I'm a strong boy, but even I couldn't haul part of a corn cob down the ramp to my den.

At least I haven't got to worry about Everton playing...that might have prevented me smiling...a lot.. Thank goodness for World Cup qualifiers. No Premier League matches this week.
