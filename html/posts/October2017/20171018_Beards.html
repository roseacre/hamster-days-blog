A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

I'm keeping my beard.

18-Oct-2017 | Milku


Even if it is No Beard Day.

I don't want to malign the promoters of No Beard Day, but have they given any thought to those of us who would look ridiculous without any facial hair?

Obviously, I'm not referring to the Big Fella there...he can look weird with a beard... not wanting to malign his hair-growing abilities or anything, but he's not one of Nature's beardies.

Yesterday was a bit disruptive, one of my little humans was off sick so there was activity in the house when I expected peace. And they decided to start building a rocket right next to my cage! Don't get excited, they're not joining the space race and have no malign purposes in creating such an object. It is a cardboard projectile, but it looks good so far. And is already huge. I wouldn't mind playing in it when it's been shown off at school.

And have you guessed the word of the day? I'm hoping for a more exhilarating one tomorrow.
