A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

No need to make my own entertainment this summer.

19-Jul-2017 | Milku


There is a football tournament to watch.

It seems I don't have to make my own entertainment this summer...can't rely on my humans to keep me amused really...

I've just discovered that there will be football to watch. The Women's European Championships are taking place in the Netherlands. And England are playing Scotland tonight. I can't wait! I hope that Fara Williams plays. We do like her even if she does play for Arsenal Ladies now. Once upon a time she played for Everton Ladies and helped out at the FA Skills sessions that one of my little humans attended in Blackpool.

I'm equally excited by the idea that dinosaurs are coming to Blackpool. I know my humans are going to go to see the big beasts in Stanley Park. I just hope they don't want to bring one home as a pet. I won't share my cage and I won't share my humans...even if I could do better.
