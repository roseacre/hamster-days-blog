A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

Carpe diem.

26-Jul-2017 | Milku


I'm going to enjoy myself while I have the chance.

Today is All or Nothing Day. Apparently, it encourages us all to just 'go for it', confront our fears and do something we really want to do. I'm just looking in my mirror and convincing myself that I can handle the success. Not going to dwell on the alternatives...

I've got a little time before I get up for the evening so plenty of time to ponder what I'll do. But it is the perfect opportunity, with only one human on duty, watching, inhibiting my freedom of expression... The rest have gone off in search of some culture. I'm not complaining that they're going far afield. I'm getting a respite from them after all, but there are cultural activities available in Blackpool...They're probably going to see them another day.

And Pets@Home liked the tweet I sent them about my new bed...not enough to re-tweet it though...
