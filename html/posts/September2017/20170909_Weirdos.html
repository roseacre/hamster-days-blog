Wonderful Weirdos.

09-Sep-2017 | Milku


I'm sure we all know one or two...

Wonderful Weirdos' Day. It's officially today...but could be an almost daily event in Blackpool. You can see some amazing people in the town... And our house has a couple of them too...not me, I hasten to add.

All's right with the world today...at least until the final football score's come in. I do hope Everton manage to beat Tottenham...even if some of those Spurs fans are poor losers... It's not looking good at half time though. How could the Toffees have let Spurs score two goals at Goodison?

My little humans have enjoyed their first days back at school and are delighted that they haven't got much homework yet.

The big fella is home again after being away all week. He's the one who keeps looking in at me during the night. It does give me a bit of a shock each time he appears, but I suppose it is nice that he wants to spend some time with me. Just as long as it's looking but not touching...
