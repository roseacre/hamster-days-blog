A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

Parma violet ice cream!

01-Sep-2017 | Milku


And a whole lots of fun at Apple Jacks..

My humans have just been to Apple Jacks near Warrington. They've had lots of fun.

There's zip wires, inflatable games and slides, go karts and more.

My gang ventured into both mazes...once made from fences and another huge maize one. And found their way through both! I'm amazed. They don't seem to have any sense of direction...unless they're heading between the sofa and kitchen...

They even tried out a new ice cream flavour. I've never heard of parma violet flavour ice cream before...and the ice cream vendors on Blackpool promenade have a vast range of ice creams... Apparently, it was delicious and tasted just like the sweets. But I can't comment on the truth of that having tasted neither.

It's almost the end of the school holidays...and a welcome return to silent days in our house. Hurrah!
