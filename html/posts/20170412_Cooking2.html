Cooking With Milku Part 2

12-Apr-2017 | Milku


Another suggestion for a tasty hamster treat you can make at home.

Another day, another treat. I love my humans! This time they mixed up a combination of:

  • mealworms
  • omega seed mix
  • cranberries
  • peanut butter
  • homemade yogurt

Once again, they mixed it, rolled it into small sausage shapes and baked them until firm.

The smaller humans weren't keen on the appearance of these treats. Good! I had no intention of sharing.

This time there was no messing. I didn't linger at my dish. I stuffed the snack into my cheek pouch and bolted for my den. It was delicious! I hope my humans made lots of these treats.
