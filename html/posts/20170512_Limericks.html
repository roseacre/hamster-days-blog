A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

Penning a verse...

12-May-2017 | Milku


It's making me curse!

I hope you're feeling poetic because today is National Limerick Day.

There was a young blogger indoors

Who could only type with his paws

A hamster you see

As cool as can be

But his views may not reflect yours.

Blackpool has a blogger Milku

He’s a hamster who is quite new

He loves eating snacks

Just one thing he lacks

A bedroom that’s not open to view.

Well, you've got to give me some credit for trying...I'm a hamster for goodness sake...you can't expect rhymes of too high a standard!

Would you accept as an excuse the fact that I have little affinity with Limerick? I much prefer the Irish counties of Mayo and Offaly.

Plus I ate my muse. It was a mealworm before you get too worried.
