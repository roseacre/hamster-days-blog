I need ear plugs...

02-Nov-2017 | Milku


Girls are loud!

Today is Men Make Dinner Day. And the Big Fella is joining in. He has no choice...He's in charge of a house-full of girls because my little humans have all invited friends to visit.

I hope he doesn't forget to cater for me too as I won't be able to make my requests heard over the noise here today.

I'd help him in the kitchen, but I really need to have a good sleep today...ready to be up supporting Everton as they play Lyon again... I might need to share the Big Fella's beer to drown my sorrows. The Toffees could be knocked out of the Europa League if they lose.
