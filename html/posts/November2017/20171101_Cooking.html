A blog from a hamster cage

Cook for your pets!

01-Nov-2017 | Milku


The way to a hamster's heart is through his stomach.

Today is Go, Cook For Your Pets Day. Please note that his should not be confused with "Cook Your Pets Day", which is popular in some parts of the world...

I was hoping the Big Fella would bake some treats... but the gang are all heading out for the afternoon and there's no aroma of freshly-baked hamster food filtering into my cage. I'm disappointed...but also delighted by the prospect of some peace and quiet.

My favourite human was visiting London yesterday and brought me back a photo of a squirrel she saw in Regent's Park. She let it get very close...I'm jealous.

I think you'll agree that it's not as cute as me.
