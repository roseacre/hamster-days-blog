Pens! They're the best friends you can have.

03-Nov-2017 | Milku


Everything I know about people I learned from pens.

Today is Fountain Pen Day. Such pens are popular with my humans. I often think they're on the same wavelength as the League of Gentlemen character who loved her pens...I find them a bit tricky to hold given the size of my paws so I'll stick with using a keyboard...

But I won't be typing too much today...far too upset...

I recovered easily from the surfeit of girls in the house yesterday, but then the goals started pouring into the wrong net...Maybe it's Everton's year to win the FA Cup. They last did that in 1995 when their league form was a bit poor...
