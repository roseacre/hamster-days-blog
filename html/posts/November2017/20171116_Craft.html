A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

We're getting crafty.

16-Nov-2017 | Milku


And helping to stop bullies.

It's Thursday so, for one of my little humans, that means it's craft day. She goes to an after school class which teaches her different skills. She's anticipating making a bookmark this evening. I don't think I'm telling any tales when I say she'll be giving it to someone as a Christmas present.

I do hope her efforts are better than something I came across on Wiki-How. Can you really tell me that the following picture looks like a pet hamster?

I wouldn't come out of my den if I looked like that! Oh, hang on, I try not to come out of my den when anyone's looking so I suppose my appearance doesn't matter...

My littlest human is probably learning more about how appearances don't matter...and that they certainly shouldn't prompt people to make cruel comments. She's an Anti-bullying ambassador in her school and is attending a conference somewhere in Blackpool today. I can't wait to hear more about it.
