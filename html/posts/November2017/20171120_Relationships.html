Human-Animal Relationship Awareness Week

20-Nov-2017 | Milku


I try to avoid my humans, but if it's just for one week I'll try to bond with them.

This week take some time to develop relationships with your animals. It's Human-Animal Relationship Awareness Week.

My week has started off with lots of attention. I got my cage cleaned out.

I just hope my humans seek to develop our relationship by plying me with lots of treats. I need to replenish the larder that was disposed of with my bedding!

I had an great opportunity to bond with my little humans at the weekend. They all decided to have a sleepover with me... Not in my cage you understand...they bedded down alongside it. They couldn't sleep in their bedroom because it was being painted.

Now, I was tempted to run on my wheel all night to keep them awake, but I'm not like that...

I don't mean that I'm considerate. I'm just a bit too lazy to do that much exercise. Everything in moderation unless it's snacking!
