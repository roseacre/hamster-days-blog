Boom Bang-a-Bang.

14-May-2017 | Milku


Never give up on you Eurovision...the UK might win again...eventually.

I'm glad it's now National Deaf Awareness Week. I think I might need some help after listening to the Eurovision Song Contest for hours last night...at far too high a volume I might add!

The Eurovision 2017 winner was good, but I much preferred some of the other songs. I'm wondering whether there wasn't some divine intervention in the result. Come on, think about it. Portugal have never won Eurovision before yet on the day Pope Francis canonized two of the Portuguese children who saw the visions at Fatima Portugal's Eurovision fortunes suddenly change.

Anyway, it was nice to see some animals performing at Eurovision this year...what do you mean they weren't real?
