International Sculpture Day

24-Apr-2017 | Milku


It's a-maze-ing...but not around here.

Today, we're celebrating the 3rd annual International Sculpture Day. There's not much going on in England that we could visit, but hopefully it'll become more popular. We just used Lego to make a few sculptures of our own.

I think my humans wanted to set up a maze and do some experiments to see whether I could navigate through it. It's really a shame that they weren't bright enough to a-maze me! I just had to try to look impressed when they showed me a couple of Lego tunnels and bridges. It beats their previous attempts to challenge me, but they should be able to do better!
