A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

Day 29 of 30DaysWild.

29-Jun-2017 | Milku


And the struggle to find nature.

My humans are quite pleased that it's day 29 of 30 Days Wild. It means that the challenge is nearly complete. It's not that they don't like nature, but it is difficult for them to get close to it on week days during term time. They intend to #StayWild at weekends and during the holidays...they promise.

So what can I talk about today? It is International Mud Day. After all the rain we've just had, there should be plenty of mud around for my humans to interact with. But I'm not convinced that they like getting grubby, unless they're enjoying a session with the We Are Adventurers folks.

When gardening, my humans haven't spotted any fairies living at the bottom of the garden. However, something is living down there and creating interesting artwork using the leaves of the rose bushes.

If only my humans could spot the culprit. It might give them an interesting picture to share for 30 Days Wild and help them stop it happening.

The only other option, is to show you a picture of a clay owl that one of my little humans made. It isn't modelled on any of the Brown Owls or Tawny Owls that my little Brownie knows. But her Brownie pack did have a visit from some real owls recently so maybe it is a model of the snowy owl that she held.
