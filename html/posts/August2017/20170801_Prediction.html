A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

I predict a riot a result.

01-Aug-2017 | Milku


But my humans don't believe me.

My humans put some pictures beside me while I was exercising in my cardboard box...yes it's still going strong even if I always try to gnaw through the corners when I'm in it. Well, I wasn't too concerned. They keep trying to find new ways to entertain me. This time, however, they seemed to think that by choosing one of the pictures I would predict the winner of Thursday's football match between MFZ Ru탑omberok and Everton.

I've told you my dilemma before. The MFK Ruzomberok badge reminds me of Lancashire's red rose emblem. The colour red is eye-catching so naturally I stopped to look at that. And you'd think think I'd committed a heinous crime. My humans assume that I'm predicting that Ru탑omberok will win. Not at all. I have every faith in Koeman's boys. But I am an Evertonian and it doesn't always pay to expect too much. This time, I am expecting Everton to progress to the next round of the Europa League competition. Honest!

I can't wait for the match actually. It means that some of my humans will be back from another trip. The one who is staying behind had better look after me well...or I'll be telling tales!
