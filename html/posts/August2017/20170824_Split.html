A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

I'm nervous.

24-Aug-2017 | Milku


Everton are playing Hajduk Split again.

I'm always slightly nervous just before an Everton match. But it's so much worse when the team are playing in Europe. I know they have a 2 goal advantage, but that can be wiped out in minutes... Not as quickly as I can clear my treat bowl, but still far too rapidly for an ardent fan.

Given their local rivals... I can't bring myself to type their name...romped to victory against Hoffenheim yesterday there is even more pressure on Everton to win. Oh, I don't know whether to watch the game or concentrate on praying for victory.

One thing I won't be doing is participating in International Strange Music Day. All the humans who play strange music in my house are on their holidays at the moment. I'm sure they'll be indulging in their weird tunes wherever they are.
