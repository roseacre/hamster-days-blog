A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

They're going away again.

08-Aug-2017 | Milku


Hope this time they visit Hamsterley Forest.

I'm bracing myself for another disruption to my routine. Some of my humans are travelling again...and leaving me to the tender mercies of the big fella... At least it's quieter when he's in charge...just the gentle hum of his computer... and the rustle of takeaway wrappers. I won't be woken up by the sound of a vacuum cleaner that's for sure.

This time I have high hopes that my humans will visit Hamsterley Forest. With a name like that, it must be a great place. I can't wait to hear all about it.

At the very least, I hope they bring me back another present from wherever they go. They have mentioned going to some wall...belonging to someone called Hadrian...doesn't sound like a promising source of snacks...
