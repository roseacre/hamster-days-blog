A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

Somewhere over the rainbow...

26-Aug-2017 | Milku


There's a Lego unicorn...

My little humans have just enjoyed a LEGO Master Model Builder session at Brockholes. They must have worked hard. They showed me some pictures of the things they created. I especially like the unicorn.

They even brought home little Lego figures because the folks from the Lego Discovery Centre in Manchester were promoting a new Lego Ninjago film.

Tomorrow, it's Banana Lover's Day. I must confess that I do enjoy pieces of dried banana. I love that crunch! But I have never tasted a piece of fresh banana. I hope my humans head to the shops to buy some.
