A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

No need to head South to the Jurassic Coast.

08-Aug-2017 | Milku


Dinosaurs have taken over Stanley Park in Blackpool.

Today my humans bravely faced some terrible lizards today in Blackpool...and I don't mean that they visited the reptile house at the zoo or went to see Barney and his friends performing at the theatre...

Jurassic Kingdom has taken over a corner of Blackpool's Stanley Park and dinosaurs are roaring in the woods. So my humans went along to have a look. They quite enjoyed getting up close to some beasts that are even fiercer than me. But did think that there should have been a bit more vegetation around the dinosaurs to hide the frameworks holding them upright.

I think I might have been a bit scared when the dinosaurs suddenly started moving and making noises so I'm glad they didn't get me a ticket... Some children did start crying apparently...not my 3 girls though... they'd have only started wailing if the bus hadn't turned up and they'd had to walk home...
