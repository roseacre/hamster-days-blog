Sisters are doing it for themselves.

06-Aug-2017 | Milku


But probably should be doing it for each other today.

Today is Sisters' Day. Not that you'd know it in our house. My little humans haven't been enjoying each other's company more than usual. And there don't seem to be any plans for a big celebration of their sisterhood. But they're not fighting after spending 2 full weeks of the summer holidays in each other's company... So I'll be celebrating that at least.

As for my sisters...I'll never know who Pets@Home sold them too. But I hope they get an extra special treat today. I'll be thinking of them as I chew my seed cornet.
