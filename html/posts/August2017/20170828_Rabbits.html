A Hamster's Eye View of Blackpool

A blog from a hamster cage

Fire safety advice.

28-Aug-2017 | Milku


Don't buy a rabbit.

Obviously I'm biased, but I have come across yet another reason why you might not want to have a rabbit as a pet.

They are dangerous! Oh I don't mean that they might bite or scratch. That's the least of your worries. They are a real risk to the very fabric of your home.

The BBC News website has highlighted a case in the North East of England where a rabbit started a house fire by chewing through an electric cable. Stupid bunny!
