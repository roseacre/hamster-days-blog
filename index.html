We're up all night to get lucky.

If Daft Punk can do it...

Maybe I'll get lucky and finally gnaw a hole in my exercise box.

Well, it’s 10th May and you all know what that means, don’t you? Well, you should. It’s time for you to get in touch with your inner hamster…it’s ‘Stay up All Night’ Night. Actually, I’m not too sure I agree with it. I don’t really want any company. You won’t think I’m contrary if I don’t take part, will you? I feel like I might need a little nap.

I had a lot of excitement yesterday evening hearing all about the fundraiser that the Rainbows, Brownies and Guides put on for St Mary’s church. I noticed that they brought home a few things for themselves, but they didn’t think about bringing me a cake.

However, the Food Technology class in the high school did provide me with a tasty treat of savoury rice. My little humans wouldn’t have it for dinner because it contained vegetables. They don’t know what they missed. My compliments to the St Mary's Food Tech teacher…
