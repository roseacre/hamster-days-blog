May the Fourth...

be with you.

While I am a little short to be a stormtrooper, I'm big enough to wish you all a Happy Star Wars Day.

The Star Wars films are enjoyed in my house so I was delighted to find out that there's a hamster version of Star Wars available too. The directors have been helping the homeless as part of this project as the cast was recruited from the Westchester Rescued Hamster Haven. But I don't think it's worth giving up my comfortable billet to get a break in the movies so I'll have to keep waiting for stardom.

Anyway, I thought today that I'd mention that I enjoy yoga...not quite a discussion about Yoda, but as close as you'll get I'm afraid.

I don't work my way through all the poses, but will try out a cobra occasionally. I like stretching up against a vertical surface. But I prefer my own 'sleepy hamster' pose that lets me extend horizontally, paws out in front and behind. Then you work your back paws in towards your tail and return to your normal position on all fours. What do you mean that's not your 'normal' position?
