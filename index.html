No socks, please, we're British...

or American, or whatever...

If it's not a school day, my little humans don't tend to wear socks. Maybe they're getting in touch with their inner hamster. But they're probably just too lazy to rummage through their drawers to find some.

Anyway, they should be able to go sock-less today as well. Why? Well, because it's No Socks Day.

For me, every day is No Socks Day. My toes keep wearing holes in any paw coverings so I don't bother. And some of my humans should follow my lead. They might think I don't notice, but I have spotted digits poking out of the end of some socks in my house. They should be ashamed!
