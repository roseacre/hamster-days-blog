I've got mail!

Hurrah! for Pets@Home.

I received my first letter through the post. It's probably a bit of an exaggeration to call it a letter, but Pets@Home addressed it to me and I'm thrilled.

It was an update from the Pets@Home VIP Club. Obviously, I'm a member because I am very important. And, although I mightn't like the term, I am a 'pet'. I actually think my humans signed me up because they like receiving money-off coupons rather than because they wanted me to network with other VIPs. But I'm not complaining because I received the mail and not them! I just hope they head off to the retail park and buy me something now.

Don't forget to prepare for the start of 30 Days Wild. My little humans aren't waiting for June. They're interacting with nature today. They're spending the day at a We Are Adventurers holiday club. I can't wait to hear all about it.
