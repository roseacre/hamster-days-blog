Just chillin'...

and enjoying the weekend.

I know my humans are out and about today so there'll be plenty of peace and quiet. Hurray!

The haiku I posted last week didn't really express my emotions or behaviour. No biting here! I needed a verse to highlight my introversion and my love of snacking. I had to resort to trying to put a few rhymes together myself.

Little hamster sits

In the dark waiting for them

To leave him in peace.

They haven't got the hint yet that I want to be left alone...

From sleep I awake.

Wait for the light to go off

To claim the snacks left.

Bright eyes, cheeky look,

Spies a dish of tasty treats.

Run, fill cheeks and hide.
