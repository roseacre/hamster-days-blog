Oh baby please.

Give a little respect to me.

So today is Respect Day.

It didn't start too well for me. As I went to bed I had to scamper through damp bedding. And, no, it wasn't that I'd changed my toilet habits. My water bottle had leaked! The big fella must have been a bit heavy handed when he deep cleaned my water bottle yesterday. He couldn't fix it this morning so I'm anticipating the arrival of a new bottle. I'm hoping for something a bit special...I have spotted some colourful water bottles in Pets@Home. One of those would brighten my cage...I don't want a red one, mind you...

I'm not even sure I want a blue one.

I'm still reeling from yesterday's bad result for Everton. They let in 4 goals and didn't score any! Rooney probably had his mind on today's court appearance, but someone else could've scored, surely... And Mourinho suggested that Everton are aiming for a top four finish in the Premier League! I wish! I'm just hoping to avoid the relegation scramble. Anything better would be a bonus.
